Whistle a Tune

Whistling a tune is easier than you think! Start by puckering your lips into a small “O” shape—imagine you’re sipping through a tiny straw. Next, place your tongue just behind your bottom teeth, keeping it flat. Blow air gently through the “O” until you hear a sound. Adjust your lip tightness and airflow to change pitch—tighter lips for higher notes, looser for lower. Practice a simple tune like “Twinkle, Twinkle” (C-C-G-G-A-A-G): two short blows, two longer ones, two high notes, then back down. Wet your lips if they’re dry, and don’t overthink it—relax and experiment!
